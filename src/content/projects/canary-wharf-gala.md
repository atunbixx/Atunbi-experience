Annual gala for an asset-management firm — four hundred guests, a keynote from a former cabinet minister, and a sit-down dinner in the central atrium. The brief was editorial: images that could carry the following year's annual report and the press kit, rather than a guest-album style coverage.

## What we covered

- Keynote and panel talks
- Awards presentation
- Plated dinner across forty tables
- Atmosphere, branding, room design
- Senior team portraits during the cocktail hour

A second photographer joined for the keynote and dinner so we could cover the room and the stage simultaneously. Images were turned around in two batches — twenty press selects within forty-eight hours, and the full edited gallery within ten working days.
