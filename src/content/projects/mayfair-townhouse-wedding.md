A small, late-winter wedding for thirty-two guests at a private Mayfair townhouse. Ceremony in the drawing room at three, drinks on the staircase, supper in the orangery, dancing until well past midnight. We photographed the day from the morning preparations through the last toast.

## What we covered

- Bridal preparations across two suites
- Ceremony — natural light, no flash
- Family group portraits on the staircase
- Drinks reception in the courtyard
- Plated supper, speeches, first dance

The room was dim, the candles were doing serious work, and the look the couple wanted was film-still rather than wedding-magazine. We shot mostly with available light, leaning into shadow where it served the frame.

## The standard

Every image was hand-finished by the studio over the following ten days. The final gallery was three hundred and twenty frames, delivered as both a private online gallery and a print-ready archive.
