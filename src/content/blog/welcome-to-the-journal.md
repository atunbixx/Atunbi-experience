The Journal is a slow place. We publish here when there's something worth saying — a venue we've come to love, a frame we keep coming back to, a quiet lesson from a recent shoot. Not a content calendar. Not a feed.

## What you'll find here

Three kinds of writing, mostly:

- **Field notes** — short reflections from a recent wedding, gala or portrait sitting. Light, framing, the bits we learned.
- **Venue features** — long-form pieces on the London venues that shape our work, from Mayfair townhouses to East End warehouses.
- **Behind the lens** — process posts on how we plan, shoot and finish a body of work.

## Why we're starting it

Two reasons. First, because writing forces clarity, and clarity makes for better work. Second, because the people who come to us tend to care about the *how* — the considered choices that separate a finished gallery from a phone dump.

If any of this is useful to you while planning your own event, that's the bonus.

— Atunbi
